$$ \frac{dp}{d\rho} = \kappa \cdot \frac{K_B \cdot T}{m} \Rightarrow \boxed{v_{Ph} = \sqrt{\kappa\cdot\frac{K_BT}{m}}}$$\\

$ v_{Ph} = v_{Ph}(\kappa,T,m) $\\
